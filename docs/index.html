Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI Writer Pro work?

AI Writer Pro adds a floating button to every website you visit. Click it, type or paste your text, choose an action (rewrite, summarize, etc.), and get instant AI-powered results. No switching tabs or copying between apps.

Which websites does it work on?

All of them. Gmail, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, WhatsApp Web, Slack, Notion, Google Docs, WordPress, Reddit, YouTube comments — any website with text inputs. The floating button appears everywhere.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your text is sent directly to the AI provider over encrypted connections. We do not store, log, or train on your content. Your API key stays in your browser's local storage.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gets you 20 AI requests per day with all actions. Pro ($9/mo) gives you 500 requests/day, priority processing speed, and custom prompt support.

Can I use my own API key?

Yes! You can bring your own OpenAI or Anthropic API key and use it directly in the extension. The subscription plans use our managed API for convenience.
